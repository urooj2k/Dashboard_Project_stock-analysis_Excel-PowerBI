# This project analyzes Adani Enterprises stock data using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Power BI

# Key KPIs

Average Close Price: 2.68K

Average 52-Week High: 3.91K

Average 52-Week Low: 1.14K

Total Volume: 0.88B

# Visualizations & Insights
1. Monthly Trading Volume

Displays sum of volume by month.

Insight: August recorded the highest trading activity (0.14B).

2. Open vs Close Monthly Trends

Shows daily totals for open and close prices.

Insight: Price trends show significant fluctuations with noticeable peaks.

3. Quarterly 52-Week High vs Low

Compares sum of 52-week highs and lows across quarters.

Insight: Q4 recorded the highest total 52-week high, indicating strong performance.

4. Monthly Volatility

Highlights market activity and liquidity by month.

Insight: May shows the highest volatility, indicating increased market movement.

# Conclusion

Volume Trends: May and August experienced peak trading volumes.

Quarterly Performance: Q4 showed the strongest stock performance.

Daily Trends: Trading activity reflects market closures on weekends.

Market Stability: Open and Close prices indicate relatively stable investor confidence
